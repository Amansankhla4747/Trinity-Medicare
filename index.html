Journey through
Hair Transplant Process

Get a step-by-step overview of the Hair Transplant Process experience at Trinity Medicare.
Learn what to expect from your consultation to post-treatment care

Step 1

Consultation and
preparation

During this stage, you'll meet with our experienced hair transplant surgeon for a personalized consultation. We'll discuss your hair restoration goals, assess your candidacy for the procedure, and create a customized treatment plan tailored to your unique needs.

Step 2

Donor graft
extraction

In this stage, donor hair follicles are carefully extracted from the back or sides of your scalp, known as the donor area. Our skilled surgeon utilizes advanced extraction techniques, such as FUE or FUT, to harvest healthy grafts while ensuring minimal scarring and maximum graft survival.

Step 3

Graft
preservation

After extraction, the harvested grafts are meticulously examined and sorted to ensure their viability and quality. Specialized preservation methods are employed to maintain the integrity of the grafts until they are ready for implantation, ensuring optimal results.

Step 4

Graft
implantation

During this stage, the extracted grafts are strategically implanted into the recipient sites on the scalp, where hair loss has occurred. Our surgeon employs precise techniques to ensure natural-looking results, paying close attention to the angle, density, and direction of hair growth.

Step 5

Post-op care
instructions

After the transplant, you'll get detailed instructions for post-op care. This covers managing discomfort, caring for the area, and avoiding activities that could affect healing. We're here to address any questions or concerns you have during your recovery.
